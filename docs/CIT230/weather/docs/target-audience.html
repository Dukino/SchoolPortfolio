Target Audience

The target audience for this website is for any user that is interested in checking the weather on a quick and easy format. The website is suitable for users who have mobile devices as well as those who are more suited to using a larger device like a laptop or tablet.

Personas

Gerald Lawson

Gerald is a senior citizen with grandchildren who he visits and travels with often. He suffers from a type of arthritis that makes it difficult to be out in cold or wet weather. He likes to take his grandchildren to the park and needs to be able to know if his joints can handle it that day.

Nicole Jackson

Nicole is a senior in college studying Communications. She drives to class and work but likes to plan her outfits in advance to make her the most comfortable when she has to walk from class to class. Her outfit needs to be work appropriate for her job but also warm and comfortable enough to be worn in the cold Idaho winter.

Scenarios

Scenario 1 - Gerald

Gerald is wanting to take his grandkids to the park on Saturday. It has been raining for the last few days and he wants to know if it will clear up by Saturday. He boots up his old computer and goes to RainOrShineNow.com. He sees immediately that the weather on Saturday will be cloudy so he writes himself a note to make sure his grandkids have a jacket when they leave the house.

Scenario 2 - Gerald

Gerald's daughter calls one afternoon to say her son has a soccer game near Gerald's house tonight. She wants to know if he will be able to make it or not. Gerald says he will be able to go as long as the weather holds up. He tells her that he will call her back and let her know if he won't be able to make it because of the weather. He hangs up and references the RainOrShineNow.com website to see what the weather will be like. He sees the weather will be fine for his arthritis and happily gets ready to leave.

Scenario 3 - Gerald

Gerald notices some unexpected clouds rolling in one afternoon. He wants to water his flowers that day to keep them healthy but knows that if it rains he won't have to. He opens RainOrShineNow.com on his phone to quickly check what the updated weather report is. Turns out there is supposed to be some rain overnight. Gerald decides he won't have to water his plants because of the constantly updating app and prepares to stay in for the night.

Scenario 4 - Nicole

Nicole wakes up at 8am to get ready for work and her classes for the day. As she looks through her closet, she sees a sweater that she wants to wear. It's starting to get warmer outside lately and she worries it will be too hot for the sweater. She opens her phone to check RainOrShineNow.com and sees that it will be in the mid-60s with a lot of sun that day. She chooses a different shirt instead.

Scenario 5 - Nicole

As Nicole is driving to class, she sees some dark clouds in the distance and a couple droplets on her windshield. She parks outside of her first class of the day, opens her phone, and checks RainOrShineNow.com. She sees that in two hours it is supposed to rain. She grabs the extra coat and umbrella she keeps in her car for her long walk to her next class after this one.
